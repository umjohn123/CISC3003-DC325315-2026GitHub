Our History

Team Synergy was formed in 2025 when Lam Ka Wai and Chao Wen Jie, both third‑year computer science students at the University of Macau, discovered their complementary skills and shared vision for ethical, impactful technology. Lam brings strong analytical and project management abilities; Wen Jie adds enterprise experience and deep AI/LLM expertise. Since then, we have collaborated on four major academic projects, participated in big data tutoring, and built a growing portfolio of real‑world prototypes. Our history is rooted in curiosity, mutual respect, and the belief that two passionate developers can achieve more together.
